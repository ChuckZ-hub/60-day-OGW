Around The Nature

At Eco Adventure Tours, our mission is to offer unforgettable travel experiences that respect and protect the planet. We are dedicated to sustainable tourism, ensuring our adventures leave minimal impact and actively contribute to the well-being of local communities.

Our tours are designed to immerse you in the natural world while educating you on the importance of conservation. Join us in exploring the beauty of our planet in a way that preserves it for future generations.

Join us in discovering the beauty of our planet, and let's work together to preserve it for generations to come. With Eco Adventure Tours, your travels make a difference.

Eco Adventure Tours typically operates in countries known for their natural beauty and commitment to sustainable tourism. These destinations offer diverse environments and wildlife, making them ideal for eco-adventures that emphasize sustainability and conservation.


Here are some countries that are often popular for eco-friendly adventure tours:

Kenya: Known for its wildlife safaris and stunning landscapes, including the Maasai Mara and Amboseli National Park.
Tanzania: Offers incredible wildlife experiences in the Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, and Mount Kilimanjaro.
Costa Rica: Famous for its biodiversity, rainforests, and eco-lodges, as well as activities like zip-lining and wildlife watching.
Brazil: Features the Amazon Rainforest, Pantanal wetlands, and beautiful coastal areas.

South Africa: Provides a range of eco-adventures, including safaris in Kruger National Park and exploring the Cape Winelands.
Nepal: Known for trekking in the Himalayas, including the Everest Base Camp and Annapurna Circuit.
Australia: Offers eco-tours in the Great Barrier Reef, Daintree Rainforest, and Tasmania.
Peru: Includes eco-tours to Machu Picchu and the Amazon Basin.

New Zealand: Renowned for its stunning landscapes, including Fiordland, Rotorua, and the Southern Alps.
Thailand: Features eco-tours in areas like Chiang Mai and the Andaman Sea, focusing on marine conservation and cultural experiences.
Canada: Provides eco-friendly tours in national parks like Banff and Jasper, and along the rugged coastline of British Columbia.

Sustainable Travel Tips

  • Choose Eco-Friendly Accommodations
  • Look for hotels and lodges with certifications for sustainability.
  • Minimize Waste
  • Carry reusable water bottles, bags, and cutlery to avoid single-use plastics.
  • Support Local Communities
  • Buy locally made products and use local services to boost the economy.
  • Travel Light
  • Pack only what you need to reduce carbon emissions from transporting heavier luggage.
